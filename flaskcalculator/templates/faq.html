When do I have to pay my fees?
If you are a domestic student and are using HECS-HELP or FEE-HELP to take out a loan on your study, the amounts payable will accrue interest and will have to be paid back once you reach a certain income threshold. More details can be found here: https://www.studyassist.gov.au/paying-back-your-loan/loan-repayment. If you are an international student or a full fee paying student, your tuition fees will be due on the first day of the teaching period and must be paid by the end of week 1 of semester.
