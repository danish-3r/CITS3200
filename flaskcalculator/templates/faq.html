If fees are not paid on time, the student will risk losing their place in the course of their choice.

If you classify as a domestic student, you can apply for a loan (HECS-HELP, FEE-HELP), which can be paid back at a later date, or pay all your units upfront every semester. International students must pay for their units in advance at the onset of every semester.

If study type or units are altered, the total fees will have to be recalculated.

An electronic statement of account can be found on your studentConnect account, outlining how much is paid and how much is still owed.

Voluntary contributions can be made to HELP loans at any time. International and full fee paying students not supported by a HELP loan cannot do partial payments, and must pay for all the units in a semester ahead of time.

Commonwealth supported students are expected to pay back their loans once they surpass a given income threshold. International and full fee paying students not supported by a HELP loan must pay for all units by the first day of tuition.
